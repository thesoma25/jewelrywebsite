Product Information

Our jewelry collection features a variety of high-quality materials including sterling silver, 14k and 18k gold, gold vermeil, and stainless steel. We also use genuine gemstones, freshwater pearls, and cubic zirconia in our designs. All materials are carefully sourced and tested to ensure they meet our quality standards.

Many of our products are hypoallergenic, particularly those made from sterling silver, stainless steel, and pure gold. However, individual sensitivities can vary. If you have specific allergies, please check the product description for detailed material information or contact our customer service team for assistance.

Yes, we offer custom jewelry design services for special occasions like engagements, weddings, and anniversaries. Our design team works closely with you to create unique pieces that reflect your personal style. Please contact us through our Contact page to discuss your custom jewelry needs.

Orders & Payments

We accept various payment methods including credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. All transactions are processed securely through encrypted connections to protect your financial information.

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can also track your order by logging into your account and visiting the Order Tracking page. Enter your order number and email address to get real-time updates on your shipment status.

We process orders quickly to ensure fast delivery. If you need to modify or cancel your order, please contact our customer service team immediately. We can usually accommodate changes if the order hasn't been processed yet. Once an order has shipped, it cannot be modified or canceled, but you can return it according to our return policy.

Shipping & Delivery

Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days within the continental United States. Expedited shipping options (2-day and overnight) are also available at checkout. International shipping times vary by destination, usually taking 7-14 business days. Please note that custom orders may require additional processing time.

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. International customers may be responsible for customs duties, taxes, and import fees imposed by their country's regulations. These charges are not included in our shipping fees and will be collected by the delivery carrier or customs office.

All orders over $100 include complimentary shipping insurance to protect against loss or damage during transit. For orders under $100, shipping insurance can be added at checkout for a small additional fee. We recommend adding insurance for all jewelry purchases to ensure your items are protected.

Returns & Exchanges

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items in their original, unworn condition with all tags and packaging intact. Custom-made items, sale items, and intimate accessories (like earrings) may have different return restrictions for hygiene reasons. Please refer to our detailed return policy for specific information.

To initiate a return or exchange, log into your account and go to the order history section. Select the order containing the item you wish to return and follow the return instructions. Alternatively, contact our customer service team for assistance. You'll receive a return authorization and shipping instructions. Returns must be initiated within 30 days of receiving your order.

For standard returns, customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item is defective or we made an error in your order. For exchanges and defective items, we provide a prepaid return shipping label. We recommend using a trackable shipping method for all returns to ensure your package can be traced if needed.

Account & Privacy

While creating an account is not required, we recommend it for a better shopping experience. With an account, you can track orders, save your shipping information, create wishlists, and enjoy faster checkout for future purchases. You can also check out as a guest if you prefer not to create an account.

We take data security seriously. Our website uses SSL encryption to protect your personal and payment information. We never share your data with third parties except as necessary to fulfill your order (such as with shipping carriers). For complete details on how we collect, use, and protect your information, please review our Privacy Policy.

You can update your account information by logging into your account and navigating to the "Account Settings" or "Profile" section. From there, you can modify your personal details, change your password, update shipping addresses, and manage payment methods. If you need assistance, our customer service team is available to help.

Product Care

To keep your jewelry looking its best, we recommend the following care tips:

  • Remove jewelry before showering, swimming, or exercising
  • Avoid contact with perfumes, lotions, and chemicals
  • Store pieces separately to prevent scratching
  • Clean regularly with a soft, lint-free cloth
  • For silver jewelry, use a silver polishing cloth to prevent tarnishing

Different materials may require specific care methods, so check the product description for detailed care instructions.

Yes, we offer professional cleaning and repair services for jewelry purchased from our store. Basic cleaning is complimentary for the lifetime of your jewelry. Repair services are available at reasonable rates depending on the type of repair needed. Contact our customer service team to arrange for cleaning or repair services.

If your jewelry becomes damaged, please stop wearing it to prevent further damage and contact our customer service team. Depending on the type of damage and how long you've owned the piece, it may be covered under our warranty. We can assess the damage and provide repair options. For items still under warranty, repairs may be complimentary.
