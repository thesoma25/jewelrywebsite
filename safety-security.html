Safety & Security

Your security is our priority. Learn about the measures we take to protect your information and ensure safe transactions.

Secure Shopping Experience

At Glamorous Look, we are committed to providing you with a secure shopping experience. We implement various security measures to protect your personal information and ensure that your transactions are safe and secure.

Our website uses industry-standard encryption technology to protect your personal and financial information during transmission. This means that any data you share with us, such as your name, address, and payment details, is encrypted and cannot be read by unauthorized parties.

Payment Security

We understand that payment security is a top concern for our customers. That's why we've implemented the following measures:

  • •
    SSL Encryption: Our website uses Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption technology to protect your payment information during transmission.
  • •
    PCI Compliance: We adhere to the Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI DSS), which is a set of security standards designed to ensure that all companies that accept, process, store, or transmit credit card information maintain a secure environment.
  • •
    Secure Payment Gateways: We use trusted and secure payment gateways to process your transactions, ensuring that your payment information is handled securely.
  • •
    No Storage of Full Payment Details: We do not store your complete credit card information on our servers. This minimizes the risk of your payment details being compromised.

Account Security

Protecting your account is essential for a safe shopping experience. Here are some measures we've implemented to ensure your account security:

  • •
    Secure Password Storage: We use industry-standard hashing algorithms to store your passwords securely. This means that even our staff cannot see your actual password.
  • •
    Account Activity Monitoring: We monitor account activities for suspicious behavior and will alert you if we detect any unusual activity on your account.
  • •
    Automatic Logout: For your security, we automatically log you out of your account after a period of inactivity.

We recommend that you also take the following steps to protect your account:

  • •
    Use Strong Passwords: Create a unique, strong password for your Glamorous Look account that includes a mix of letters, numbers, and special characters.
  • •
    Keep Your Password Confidential: Never share your password with anyone, including our customer service representatives.
  • •
    Be Cautious of Phishing: Be wary of emails or messages asking for your account information. Glamorous Look will never ask for your password via email or phone.

Secure Shipping and Delivery

We understand that receiving your jewelry and accessories safely is important to you. Here's how we ensure secure shipping and delivery:

  • •
    Trusted Shipping Partners: We work with reputable shipping carriers to ensure that your packages are handled with care and delivered securely.
  • •
    Discreet Packaging: We ship all items in discreet packaging to protect your privacy and prevent theft.
  • •
    Tracking Information: We provide tracking information for all orders so you can monitor the status and location of your package.
  • •
    Delivery Confirmation: We require delivery confirmation for all orders to ensure that your package has been delivered to the correct address.
  • •
    Insurance Options: For high-value items, we offer shipping insurance to protect your purchase during transit.

Fraud Prevention

We have implemented several measures to prevent fraud and protect both our customers and our business:

  • •
    Address Verification System (AVS): We use AVS to verify that the billing address provided matches the address associated with the credit card.
  • •
    Card Verification Value (CVV): We require the CVV code for all credit card transactions to verify that the person making the purchase has physical possession of the card.
  • •
    Fraud Detection Systems: We use advanced fraud detection systems to identify and prevent suspicious transactions.
  • •
    Manual Review: Our team manually reviews orders that are flagged as potentially fraudulent to ensure that legitimate orders are not declined.
